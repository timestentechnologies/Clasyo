5. Getting Started

For School Administrators

  1. Complete the school registration process
  2. Set up your school profile and academic calendar
  3. Add staff, teachers, and students to the system
  4. Configure academic terms, classes, and subjects
  5. Set up the fee structure and payment methods

For Teachers

  1. Log in with your credentials
  2. Access your class schedule and student lists
  3. Record attendance and grades
  4. Create and grade assignments
  5. Communicate with students and parents

For Students & Parents

  1. Log in with your provided credentials
  2. View your class schedule and assignments
  3. Check grades and attendance
  4. Access study materials and resources
  5. View fee statements and payment history
